# **Data-Analytics-on-Olist-E-Commerce-Platform-with-Excel**


## Project Overview
This project aims to analyze the performance of **Olist – Soluções de Vendas Online e Serviços de E-commerce** by transforming raw operational data into clear and actionable business insights. The analysis is conducted using Excel and focuses on three main areas: **Customer Analysis, Delivery Performance, and Product Performance**.

Olist serves nearly 100,000 customers across Brazil, with transaction activity highly concentrated in São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro. While overall growth is strong, challenges remain in uneven regional distribution, delivery delays and undelivered orders, as well as high logistics costs in certain product categories. This project addresses these challenges by defining key metrics, tracking performance trends, and highlighting optimization opportunities.

## Objectives
- Understand customer distribution, growth trends, payment behavior, and purchase value.
- Evaluate delivery performance including order status, delivery timeliness, and regional logistics efficiency.
- Analyze product performance based on sales volume, purchase value, customer reviews, and freight costs.
- Support data-driven decision-making to improve operational efficiency, customer experience, and profitability.

## Key Analysis Areas
### Customer Analysis
- Customer distribution by city and state  
- Customer growth over time  
- Payment type comparison  
- Average purchase value  

### Delivery Performance
- Orders by order and delivery status  
- Delivered and undelivered orders  
- On-time and late delivery  
- Average delivery time  
- Order purchase timestamp  
- Orders by city and state  

### Product Performance
- Average product review score  
- Best-selling products  
- Products with the highest purchase value  
- Products with the best freight value  
- Best-performing product categories  

## Expected Deliverables
The project delivers an **interactive Excel dashboard** that visualizes key customer, delivery, and product metrics. A simple dashboard mockup is used to define chart structures, KPIs, and filters before final implementation. In addition, a short analytical report and a basic data pipeline documentation are provided to ensure clarity, consistency, and ease of data refresh.

## Project Timeline
The project follows three main milestones:
1. **Data Preparation & KPI Definition** – Data cleaning and KPI finalization  
2. **Dashboard Development** – Building Excel dashboards with charts, KPIs, and filters  
3. **Review & Final Delivery** – Validation, refinement, and final insight delivery  

## Stakeholders & Contributors
**Stakeholders**
- Business Owner / Management (Approver)  
- Operations / Logistics Manager (Informee)  
- Sales / Marketing Team (Informee)  

**Contributors**
- Data Analyst – Data cleaning, KPI definition, dashboard development, and insights  
- Data Source Provider – Transaction, customer, delivery, and product data  
- Business Representative – Business context validation  

## Tools
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables & Charts  

## Outcome
This project provides a structured and easy-to-understand analytical dashboard that helps stakeholders identify performance gaps, optimize logistics and product strategies, and improve overall customer experience in a scalable and sustainable way.
